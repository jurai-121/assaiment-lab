Command:
./patterns.sh
Explanation:
The script reads words from input.txt, converts them to lowercase for case-insensitive checking, and classifies them using regular expressions.

Command:
grep -Eq '^[aeiou]+$'
Explanation:
This pattern matches words containing only vowels.

Command:
grep -Eq '^[bcdfghjklmnpqrstvwxyz]+$'
Explanation:
This pattern matches words containing only consonants.

Command:
grep -Eq '^[bcdfghjklmnpqrstvwxyz][a-z]*$'
Explanation:
This pattern identifies words starting with a consonant, and additional checks confirm mixed vowel and consonant content.
